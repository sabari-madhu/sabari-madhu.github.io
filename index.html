DID YOU KNOW???

DID YOU KNOW-

The main greenhouse gases(GHG)in the Earth's atmosphere are water vapour, carbon dioxide, methane, nitrous oxide,and ozone.
Though these gases are already present in our atmosphere, the problems begin when we distort and accelerate the natural process by releasing more GHG in the atmosphere than are necessary to maintain the planet to an ideal habitable temperature.

Contrary to popular belief that only fuel emissions contribute to emissions of GHG, every type of energy consumption, right from our electricity consumption to the whole lifecycle of products that we use in our daily lives, contribute to emission of GHG. Increasing levels of GHG in the atmosphere severely affect our environment by trapping the sun's heat and increasing the temperature. This is a major cause for concern.

Reducing emissions of GHG may seem like a daunting task, but if each one of us makes a commitment to take small steps today, we can make a big difference tomorrow. This commitment can ensure a better world for our future generations.
